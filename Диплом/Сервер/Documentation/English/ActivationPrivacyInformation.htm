Online Activation

What is Online Activation?

Activating a software product from TEC-IT means that the software connects via Internet to TEC-IT's activation server, sends the data listed below to this activation server and receives a license key from the activation server. This license key is then used to unlock the product permanently.
The sole purpose of the activation server is to distribute licenses to customers and to keep track regarding the number of activations for a specific activation key. Thus the activation server is usually only required once. After the product was licensed no Internet connectivity is required.

Which Information is sent to TEC-IT?

When activating a software product the following information is sent to TEC-IT and maybe stored permanently:

  • Activation Key
    This activation key was sent to you by TEC-IT or a reseller of TEC-IT. No personal (or any other encrypted) information is contained within this activation key.
  • Company Name, Country, ZIP and City
    This data is required to assign you the purchased license.
  • Email Address
    The email address is used to send you a copy of your license key. TEC-IT treats your email address confidential (see below).
  • Product IDs
    This product IDs are used to identify the product which you are activating.
  • System ID
    The System ID identifies the computer on which the product is activated. TEC-IT uses either the volume serial number, the computer's serial number, or the computer's host-name (the name of the computer within your network) to identify a system.
  • Product Names
    Localized descriptions of the product and the available license variants.
  • IP Address, Date and Time
    The IP address of your computer (or Internet gateway) as well as the current date and time are stored for logging purposes.

Does TEC-IT Disclose Information?

In general information like company name, city, zip, state, email, ... is treated strictly confidential and only disclosed to third party in the following circumstances:

  • If TEC-IT is required to do so by law
  • For the purpose of order processing
    E.g. forwarding required data to payment providers.
  • If you agree to disclose your personal information.
    Please note: we will ask you beforehand (e.g. if you would like to serve as a reference customer)!

Manual Activation (no Internet)

Can I use an Alternative Licensing Method?

Yes. TEC-IT also provides an alternative method for licensing a product - just get in touch with us. This alternative method does not require Internet connectivity. The System-ID is required in some cases.
